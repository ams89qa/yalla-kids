1. Acceptance of terms

By creating an account, making a booking, or otherwise using yallakids.qa (the "Service"), you agree to these Terms of Service and to our Privacy Policy. If you don't agree, please don't use the Service.

Yalla Kids is operated by Yalla Kids Technologies W.L.L., a company registered in Doha, Qatar.

2. What we do

Yalla Kids is a marketplace that connects parents in Qatar with classes, camps, venues, and experiences for their children. We verify providers, publish listings, and facilitate bookings and payments. We are not the provider of any class or experience listed on the platform — the provider is.

3. Your account

You must be at least 18 years old and a parent, guardian, or legally responsible adult to create an account. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials safe and for all activity on your account. Let us know immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.

When registering a child on your account, you confirm that you are the parent or legal guardian, or have permission from them.

4. Bookings

When you book a class or experience through Yalla Kids, you enter a contract directly with the provider. We facilitate the booking and payment, but the provider is responsible for delivering the service.

You must provide accurate information about your child (age, allergies, medical notes, pickup authorization). Inaccurate information may affect the provider's ability to keep your child safe, and may void our refund guarantees.

5. Payments & refunds

Payments are processed by MyFatoorah, a licensed payment provider in the State of Qatar. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, and NAPS. All prices are in Qatari Riyals (QAR) and include any applicable taxes.

Refund policy

  • Full refund if you cancel at least 7 days before the term start date.
  • 50% refund if you cancel between 48 hours and 7 days before the term start date.
  • No refund after the first session of the term has taken place, except where the provider cancels the class or materially fails to deliver it.
  • If a provider cancels, you get a full refund automatically.

Processing fees (displayed as a line item at checkout) are non-refundable. Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 7–14 business days.

Safety-related refunds: If we de-list a provider for a verified safety issue mid-term, we refund the unused portion of your booking in full, regardless of when you cancel.

6. Providers

Providers are independent third parties. We vet them — trade license, insurance, staff permits — but we do not supervise day-to-day operations. Disputes about the quality of a class should first be raised with the provider. If you can't resolve it directly, contact us and we'll help mediate.

Providers listed as "Verified" have passed our baseline checks. The "Featured" and "Premium" labels indicate a commercial tier and do not imply a higher safety standard.

  • Post false, defamatory, or deliberately misleading reviews.

  • Use the Service for any unlawful purpose under Qatari law.
  • Scrape, copy, or republish listing data without our permission.
  • Impersonate another person or misrepresent your relationship to a child.

We may remove content or suspend accounts that violate these rules.
