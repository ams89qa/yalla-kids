Answers to the questions parents (and providers) ask us most. Can't find it? Email hello@yallakids.qa — a real human will reply.

Account & login

Do I need an account to browse?
No — you can browse everything without an account. You only need one to book, save favorites, and leave reviews.
How do I sign up?
Email + password, or continue with Google / Apple. Takes under a minute. We never post anywhere on your behalf.
I forgot my password.
Click "Sign in → Forgot password" and we'll email you a reset link within a minute. Check spam if you don't see it.
Can I add multiple kids to one account?
Yes — add as many as you need from your dashboard. Each kid's age, allergies, and emergency contact are stored separately. Two parents can share one account.

Bookings & classes

How far in advance can I book a class?
Usually 6–8 weeks before term start. Popular classes fill up 2 weeks before. We'll email you when next-term bookings open for classes you've saved.
Can I switch from one class to another mid-term?
Most providers allow a switch with 48-hour notice, subject to availability. Message the provider directly via their profile page.
What if my kid wants to drop out after the first class?
If it's within 7 days of term start, full refund. After that, 50% up to 48h before the second class. After the second class, no refund (but we'll always try to find a make-up or credit).
What if a class is cancelled by the provider?
100% refund to your card, automatically, within 5 business days. No action needed from you.

Payment & refunds

What payment methods do you accept?
All major cards (Visa, Mastercard), Apple Pay, Google Pay, and NAPS — all via MyFatoorah. We don't accept cash deposits to providers.
Is my card information stored?
Not by Yalla Kids. MyFatoorah handles card tokenization securely. We never see your full card number.
Will I be charged in QAR or my home currency?
All prices on Yalla Kids are in QAR. If your card is in another currency, your bank will convert at its daily rate.
How long do refunds take?
Typically 3–5 business days to show in your account. Longer for international cards.
Can I get an invoice for my employer?
Yes — every receipt in your dashboard has a "Download invoice" button. Need custom invoice fields? Message hello@yallakids.qa.

Kids & safety

How do you verify providers are safe?

Before a provider can list, we check:

• Qatar commercial registration (CR) number
• Valid liability insurance
• Work permits for all staff
• Venue safety & accessibility
• Reference checks from 2 prior clients

Providers with the ✓ Verified badge have completed this process. Featured and Premium providers undergo an annual re-check.

What happens if something goes wrong during a class?
Report the incident to us within 72 hours via your dashboard or email disputes@yallakids.qa. We investigate within 48 hours and, where appropriate, issue a full refund + escalate with the provider. Safety issues may result in the provider being removed from the platform.
Can I request a female-only or male-only class?
Yes. Many providers offer single-gender classes — filter by "Girls only" or "Boys only" on any listing page. Listings that are open to all are labeled "Mixed."

Arabic & language

Is Yalla Kids bilingual?
Yes — Arabic and English throughout. Toggle any page in the top-right. Emails, SMS, and receipts also go in your chosen language.
Are all providers bilingual?
No — each class listing shows its teaching language clearly. Many are bilingual; some are AR-only or EN-only. Filter by language on any search page.

For providers

How do I list my business?
Start at our For Providers page and fill out the application. Verification takes 1–3 business days.
How much does it cost?
Classes: 10% commission on bookings, no monthly fee. Places: free tier available, paid tiers from QAR 99/month. See full pricing.
When do I get paid?
Payouts twice monthly (1st and 15th) by bank transfer. Premium providers receive weekly payouts.

Other

Is Yalla Kids available in other cities / countries?
We're Doha-focused for now. Al Khor, Al Wakrah, and Lusail listings are live. Expansion to the UAE and KSA is on the roadmap for 2027.
Do you have a mobile app?
Our website is mobile-first and works great as a PWA — "Add to Home Screen" on Safari or Chrome. A native iOS & Android app is coming in 2027.
How can I suggest a place or provider?
Email tips@yallakids.qa with the business name and a link. We aim to review every tip within 5 days. Thank you — the platform grows because of parents like you.

Still stuck?

Real humans at hello@yallakids.qa answer within 24 hours (Sat–Thu).
