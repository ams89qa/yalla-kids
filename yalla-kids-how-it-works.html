How it works

Yalla Kids in four steps — for parents. Provider? Start here instead.

For parents For providers
01

Discover — whether you want a class, a place, or a plan

Search by age, neighborhood, or mood. Our editors keep the guides honest and the ratings real. Filter by AC, free, stroller-friendly, language, or halal food — the filters parents actually want.

💡 Tip: save your go-to's to My Favorites. Coming back to book is one tap.
02

Compare & decide — every listing shows what matters

Ratings from real Qatar parents. Honest pricing in QAR (never "call for price"). AC-status. Prayer-room info. Stroller access. Photos taken by editors or verified providers — not stock images from the other side of the world.

✓ Every provider is verified before they can list. Safety first, gimmicks never.
03

Book securely — pay through MyFatoorah

Classes book directly on Yalla Kids. Places that charge entry (OliOli, Angry Birds, Mathaf guided tours) also bookable. One checkout, Arabic or English, with Visa/Mastercard, Apple Pay, or NAPS. No card on file, no surprise charges, no shady cash deposits.

🔒 Secured by MyFatoorah. Full refund within 7 days of term start. 50% up to 48h before.
04

Enjoy — and leave a real review

Reminders by SMS or WhatsApp (your choice). Directions with accurate parking notes. After class, leave a review so the next parent gets the same benefit you did. Verified reviews only — you can't rate a class you didn't actually attend.

🎁 Get QAR 50 credit for every friend you refer who makes their first booking.
🛡

Safety & vetting

We check trade licenses, insurance, work permits, and venue safety before any provider goes live. Anything off, we don't list.

💰

Fair, transparent pricing

10% commission on class bookings. Free tier for every place. Never "call for price" on a listing. Ever.

🗣

Truly bilingual

Every flow — search, booking, receipts, SMS — works in Arabic and English. Switch anytime, any page.
