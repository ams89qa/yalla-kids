1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy describes how Yalla Kids Technologies W.L.L. ("Yalla Kids", "we", "us") collects and uses personal information when you use yallakids.qa. We're registered and operate from Doha, Qatar, and we process data in accordance with Qatari data protection law (Law No. 13 of 2016).

3. Why we collect it

  • To operate bookings — matching you with providers, processing payments, issuing receipts.
  • To keep kids safe — sharing allergy and pickup information with the provider your child is attending.
  • To communicate — booking confirmations, SMS reminders, WhatsApp updates (only if you opt in), service announcements.
  • To improve the Service — understanding which listings are popular, where users drop off, what filters are useful.
  • To comply with the law — tax records, consumer protection obligations, responding to lawful requests.
  • To prevent fraud — detecting suspicious logins, chargeback abuse, and fake reviews.

4. Kids' data

We treat information about children with extra care. When you add a child to your account, that information is:

  • Visible only to you inside Yalla Kids, except when you make a booking — at which point the relevant details (first name, age, allergies, emergency contact, pickup authorization) are shared with the provider for that class only.
  • Never used for advertising or profiling.
  • Never sold or rented to any third party.

Children should not create Yalla Kids accounts themselves. If you believe a child under 18 has created an account without parental consent, email privacy@yallakids.qa and we will remove it.

Medical information: Allergy and medical notes are stored encrypted and shared only with the providers delivering a class your child is enrolled in. They are removed from booking records 90 days after the booking ends.

5. Who we share data with

We share data only with parties who help us operate the Service, and only to the extent they need it:

  • Providers — name, age, allergies, and pickup info of children enrolled in their classes.
  • MyFatoorah — for payment processing. MyFatoorah is PCI-DSS compliant and regulated by the Qatar Central Bank.
  • Communication tools — email (Resend / Amazon SES), SMS (Ooredoo / Vodafone Qatar APIs), WhatsApp Business API.
  • Analytics — Plausible Analytics (privacy-first, no personal identifiers).
  • Infrastructure — our hosting provider (AWS Middle East - Bahrain region) and CDN.
  • Qatari authorities — when legally required (court order, tax audit, regulatory request).

We do not sell personal data. We do not use third-party ad networks.

6. How long we keep data

  • Account data — while your account is active, plus 12 months after closure.
  • Booking & payment records — 5 years (Qatari tax & commercial law).
  • Medical / allergy notes — removed from booking records 90 days after the booking ends. Kept on the kid profile only if you keep it there.
  • Support messages — 2 years.
  • Analytics data — aggregated, not linked to individuals, kept indefinitely.

7. Security

We encrypt data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. We restrict employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis and log all access. We run security reviews annually and after any incident.

If we ever discover a data breach that affects your personal information, we'll notify you within 72 hours and tell you what we know, what we're doing about it, and what you should do.

8. Your rights

Under Qatari data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you (self-serve in your dashboard, or request a copy).
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data (except records we must retain for tax or safety reasons).
  • Export your data in a portable format (JSON or CSV).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing messages at any time.
  • Object to certain uses of your data.
  • Complain to the Compliance and Data Protection Department at the Ministry of Transport and Communications.

To exercise any of these rights, use your dashboard settings or email privacy@yallakids.qa. We'll respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies:

  • Strictly necessary — login session, language preference, CSRF token. These can't be turned off.
  • Analytics — a first-party Plausible cookie to count page views. Anonymous, no cross-site tracking.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers. You can clear cookies anytime from your browser settings.

10. International transfers

Our servers are hosted in AWS Middle East (Bahrain). Some service providers (e.g. Resend for email) may process data outside of Qatar and the GCC. Where that happens, we rely on standard contractual clauses and encryption to protect your data.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. For material changes we'll notify you by email and post a notice on the Service at least 14 days before changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the current version.
