Local tips

What parents wish they knew before visiting The Pearl

Park at Medina Centrale, not Porto Arabia. Weekend traffic around Porto Arabia bottlenecks after 4pm. Medina's underground garage is free for 3 hours with any café purchase and the walk to the playground is shorter than it looks.

The splash pad runs Oct–May. It's turned off in peak summer to conserve water. In July–September, come before 9am for the shaded play area or head inside to the Medina Centrale kids' zone.

Stroller-friendly? Mostly. Qanat Quartier has wide flat promenades. Porto Arabia has cobblestones — bring the sturdy stroller, not the umbrella one.

FAQs about The Pearl with kidsأسئلة شائعة عن اللؤلؤة مع الأطفال

Is The Pearl walkable with a stroller?
Yes for Qanat Quartier and Medina Centrale. Porto Arabia has cobblestone sections. The whole promenade loop is about 4km.
Where do I park for free?
Medina Centrale underground, Giardino Mall, and two public lots near the Carrefour exit are all free or validated. Avoid the valet unless you enjoy waiting.
Is the water safe to swim in?
Porto Arabia is a harbor — not swimming water. For actual beach swimming, head to Katara or Simaisma.
What's open on a Friday morning?
Most shops open after 2pm, but parks and cafés are open by 9am. Perfect window for quiet playground time.
