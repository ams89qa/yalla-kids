Goal: help parents

Why we built this

Qatar has amazing things for kids. The problem isn't supply — it's discovery. Every expat parent ends up in a different WhatsApp group to learn that there's a decent art class in Al Waab or a hidden free splash pad in Katara. Nothing's indexed. Nothing's reviewed. Nothing's bookable in one place.

So we built the thing we wanted. Yalla Kids aggregates every class, place, and kid-friendly venue in Qatar into one searchable, bookable, bilingual platform. We vet every provider. We collect real parent reviews. We keep it honest.

What makes us different

🇶🇦

Built for Qatar

Every listing respects local prayer times, dress codes, and holidays. Arabic is first-class, not bolt-on. Payments go through MyFatoorah, not Stripe.

✓

Every provider vetted

We check trade licenses, insurance, staff permits, and references before a provider goes live. You see a ✓ badge only if we've actually verified it.

💬

Honest, editorial-first

Our guides are written by parents who live here, pay their own entry fees, and call out the overpriced mall chains. Sponsored content is always labeled.

How we make money (and don't)

We charge providers a 10% commission when a parent books a class through Yalla Kids. That's it. We don't sell your data, we don't accept undisclosed sponsorships, and we don't let providers pay to be labeled "Verified."

For places (museums, parks, cafés) we offer a four-tier listing model: Free (every place gets a free listing), Verified (QAR 99/mo), Featured (QAR 499/mo), and Premium (QAR 1,499/mo). The difference is placement and tools, never inflated ratings. See For providers.

Our story, short version

2023 — Austin spent a whole Saturday driving to four closed or nonexistent "soft plays" pulled from random Instagram posts. He started a spreadsheet.

2024 — The spreadsheet became a research project — every call made, every website checked, every opening-hours screenshot saved.

2025 — The research became Yalla Kids. We opened our first catalogue with 40 verified places and 66 class programmes across Doha.

2026 — We're where you are reading this now. Launching bookings, reviews, proper Arabic throughout, and weekly guides.
