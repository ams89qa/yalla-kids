Yalla Kids Collection · 25 places

25 rainy-day (well, 40°C-day) saviors in Doha. ٢٥ وجهة منقذة في الدوحة.

Every parent's shortlist of AC-cooled, kid-approved indoor spots for when the thermometer becomes an emergency. Ranked by our editors, tested with real children, sorted by age range.

YK
Yalla Kids Editorial · Updated April 12, 2026 · 14-min read
❄️🏛️✨
25 places · AC guaranteed · Ages 0–12 · Spend: Free → QAR 180

When Doha hits 40°C by 8am and every mall play area is packed with strollers, you need a Plan B. This is ours — the places we actually send parents to in our group chats when summer becomes untenable. They're ranked, but loosely; #1 is our most-recommended, and everything after is roughly equal in excellence.

01
🛝

OliOli, Aspire

Still the gold standard. Eight immersive galleries, none of them hammering on the senses, all of them beautifully maintained. The Arabic calligraphy gallery converts even the most screen-hypnotized tween for at least 20 minutes. Book the 9:30 slot — it's quieter, and crossing the indoor bridge to the ball pit first is psychologically important.

❄️ AC 👶 Ages 3–10 💰 QAR 65 / kid 🕐 90-min avg
See place → Save to trip
02
📚

Qatar National Library

The children's section is, quite honestly, one of the most beautiful public spaces in Doha. Free. Has Arabic & English storytime twice a week. The adults' café is a secret backup plan.

❄️ AC 👶 All ages 💰 Free 📖 Storytime Sat 10am EN / 11:30 AR
See place →
03
🐠

Museum of Islamic Art (MIA)

Masterfully air-conditioned, architecturally stunning, and free for residents. The Discover Islamic Art tablet trail keeps 8+ kids busy for 90 minutes. Bring headphones for the audio tour — the hall echoes.

❄️ AC 👶 Ages 2+ 💰 Free residents
See place →
04
🏛

Msheireb Museums

Four restored heritage homes, all free, all air-conditioned, with surprisingly engaging audio guides for kids 8+. Pair with an iced karak at Café #999 around the corner.

❄️ AC 👶 Ages 6+ 💰 Free

05
🎨

Mathaf Museum of Modern Arab Art

The guided family tours (Saturdays) are genuinely fun — kids make their own sketchbook to fill in. Often overlooked by parents because "museum" but it's actually built with kids in mind.

❄️ AC 👶 Ages 5+ 💰 Free for residents
See place →
06
🎪

Angry Birds World, Festival City

Loud. Bright. Unapologetically commercial. Kids 5–10 have a ball. Avoid on a Thursday or Friday evening — the queues defeat the purpose.

❄️ AC 👶 Ages 5–10 💰 QAR 160 / kid
