About the studioعن الاستوديو

Studio Canvas Doha is a bilingual art school in West Bay Lagoon teaching kids ages 4–12 how to see, imagine, and make. Our small-group classes (max 10 kids) run 8-week terms in painting, drawing, clay, and mixed media. Every child takes home a portfolio, and every term ends with a mini-exhibition.

We believe creativity is a skill, not a talent — and that every kid in Qatar deserves a place to explore it in their own language.

Provider FAQsالأسئلة الشائعة

What do kids need to bring to class?
Nothing! We provide aprons, all materials, and a portfolio folder for each child. Please dress for mess.
What if my child misses a session?
Each term includes one free make-up slot. Additional misses are forfeited, but we'll send photos of the work they missed.
Do you offer trial classes?
Yes — QAR 100 trial available before signing up for a full term. Message us to arrange.
Is the studio wheelchair accessible?
Yes. Tower 12 has step-free access and a lift. Our studio has a wide door and accessible bathroom on the same floor.
